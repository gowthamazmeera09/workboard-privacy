Privacy Policy for WorkBoard

Last Updated: 30 November 2025

WorkBoard (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information: Name, phone number, address, profile photo, work details, experience, and subscription/payment status.

b) Automatically Collected Information: Device information, Android version, approximate location, usage data, and analytics.

c) Payment Information: We use Razorpay for secure payments. We do not store card details, UPI IDs, or banking information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To show workers based on category and location
  • To manage daily and monthly worker listings
  • To unlock contact information after payment
  • To track attendance for monthly workers
  • To improve app performance and features
  • To provide customer support

3. Location Access

We use your approximate location to show nearby workers. We do not store or share your exact GPS location.

4. Data Sharing

We only share your information with:

  • Razorpay (for payments)
  • Service providers (analytics, hosting)

We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

5. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and other measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You can update your profile, request deletion of your data, or contact us for any privacy concerns.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Continued use of the App means you accept the updated policy.
