Privacy Policy for WorkBoard

Last Updated: 14 February 2026

WorkBoard ("App", "we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the WorkBoard mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Address or area details
  • Profile photo (if uploaded)
  • Work category and experience details
  • Subscription/payment status

1.2 Location Information

We collect approximate location data to display nearby workers. We do not collect or store precise GPS location unless required for app functionality.

1.3 Payment Information

Payments are processed securely through Razorpay. We do not store card numbers, UPI IDs, or bank account details on our servers.

1.4 Device & Usage Data

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App usage analytics
  • Crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect users with daily and monthly workers
  • To unlock contact details after successful payment
  • To track attendance for monthly worker subscriptions
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To provide customer support
  • To prevent fraud and misuse

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only with:

  • Razorpay (payment processing)
  • Hosting or analytics service providers
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Data Retention

We retain user information as long as the account is active or as necessary to provide services. If you request account deletion, your personal data will be deleted within 30 days unless legally required to retain it.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. However, no system can guarantee complete security.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access or update your information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Account Deletion

You may request account deletion by emailing us at support@workboardapp.in. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
